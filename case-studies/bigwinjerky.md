# BigWinJerky Case Study

## Project Type

Local product website and brand website

## Main Focus

BigWinJerky is a small-batch beef jerky brand website focused on product presentation, flavor pages, local SEO, direct ordering calls to action, and brand storytelling.

## Goals

- Present products clearly
- Build a premium local brand
- Improve local search visibility
- Create flavor pages
- Make ordering simple
- Support future growth

## Key Areas

- Product website design
- Local SEO
- Flavor pages
- Brand copywriting
- Call-to-action structure
- Mobile-friendly design

## Skills Used

- Web design
- SEO
- Product positioning
- Local business content
- Brand structure
- Conversion-focused layout

## Results

This project shows experience building a local product website with clear branding, SEO-focused content, and customer-friendly ordering paths.
