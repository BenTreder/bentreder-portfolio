# QuickTapID Case Study

## Project Type

Digital business card and NFC product website

## Main Focus

QuickTapID is a digital profile and NFC business card platform designed for small business owners, creators, service providers, and local professionals.

## Goals

- Explain the product clearly
- Build trust with potential customers
- Create industry-specific landing pages
- Support onboarding
- Improve conversion paths
- Make the product easy to understand

## Key Areas

- NFC business cards
- Digital business profiles
- Landing pages
- Product SEO
- Industry pages
- Customer onboarding

## Skills Used

- Web design
- SEO content structure
- Product positioning
- Conversion-focused copy
- Landing page design
- Technical setup

## Results

This project demonstrates experience building a product-style website that combines web design, business strategy, SEO, and customer onboarding.
